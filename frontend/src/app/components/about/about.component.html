About Urban Life Hub

Our Mission

Urban Life Hub is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between customers and businesses in urban environments. We create meaningful connections that enhance urban living through seamless service delivery and community building.

What We Offer

For Customers

Discover and connect with trusted local businesses, book services effortlessly, and enjoy secure transactions.

For Businesses

Expand your reach, streamline operations, and build your reputation with our powerful business tools.

Community Focus

We foster community engagement through reviews, recommendations, and local events.

Our Team

Urban Life Hub was created by Group 11 for the CS4116 project at the University of Limerick. Our team of dedicated developers combined their expertise in web development, user experience design, and business analytics to build this platform.
